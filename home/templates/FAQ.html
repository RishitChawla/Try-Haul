FAQ(s)

Ordering and Payments

Q: How do I place an order?

  • Select the product(s) you want to purchase.
  • Add them to your cart and proceed to checkout.
  • Enter your shipping details and make the payment.
  • You will receive a confirmation email with your order details.

Q: What payment methods are accepted?

We accept the following payment methods:

  • Credit/Debit Cards (Visa, MasterCard, etc.)
  • UPI and Net Banking
  • Digital Wallets (if applicable)
  • Cash on Delivery (if available)

Q: Can I cancel or modify my order after placing it?

Once an order is confirmed, it cannot be modified. However, you can cancel it before the order is processed by contacting our support team. After this period, cancellations may not be possible as the brand might have started processing it.

Shipping and Delivery

Q: How long does shipping take?

Shipping times vary depending on the brand and your location. On average:

  • Domestic orders: 5-10 business days

Orders may get delayed as the delivery is directly controlled by the brands onboard

Q: Do you offer international shipping?

Yes, we ship globally. Shipping rates and delivery times will vary based on the destination.

Q: How can I track my order?

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking number via email. You can use this number to track your package on the courier's website.

Returns, Refunds and Exchange

Q: What is your return policy?

At present Try&Hauls does not offer ant returns however you can apply for exchange within a period of 10 days with original tag and the product must be unused

Products and Sizing

Q: How do I know which size to order?

Each brand may have its own sizing chart. You can find size guides on the product pages. If you're unsure, reach out to us for assistance.

Q: Are the products authentic?

Yes, all products sold on Try&Hauls come directly from the brands themselves, ensuring authenticity and quality.
